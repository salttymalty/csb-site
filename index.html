What We Do

Full-Service Production

From intimate events for 5 people to large-scale festivals for 5,000+. We handle sound, backline, AV, lighting, recording, and live streaming.

🎤

Live Sound

Professional sound reinforcement for any size event. Full mixing, processing, and speaker deployment with premium Meyer Sound, L'Acoustics, and QSC systems.

🎸

Backline

Extensive inventory of professional drums, keyboards, guitars, amps, and percussion. Vintage and modern gear from top brands, maintained and stage-ready.

🎥

AV & Production

Projectors, screens, video switching, multi-camera live streaming, and lighting. Full production support for corporate events and galas.

🎙

Recording

Pro Tools-based recording services for live events and studio sessions. Multi-track capture with professional microphones and outboard processing.

📡

Wireless & IEM

Shure ULXD and QLXD wireless systems with up to 24 channels. PSM 1000 and PSM 900 in-ear monitor systems for professional monitoring.

📦

Dry Rentals

Need gear without crew? Plug-and-play rental packages with delivery and pickup. Daily, weekly, and flexible terms.
